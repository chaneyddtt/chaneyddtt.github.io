Short Bio

I am currently a fourth-year PhD student in the ECE Department at National Univeristy of Singapore, advised by Prof. Loong Fah Cheong and Prof. Gim Hee Lee. Before, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in the EEIS Department at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2015. From June to November 2018, I interned at Media Analytics Department of NEC Laboratories America, working with Manmohan Chandraker, Quoc-Huy Tran and Pan Ji.

Research Interest

3D Computer Vision in general, with emphasis on Structure from Motion and its applications to robotics and non-geometric vision.
